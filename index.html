About

About my background and research.

Hi, I am Xuying Ning. I am currently an M.S. student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where I work with Prof. Jingrui He and continue on the Ph.D. track. Before UIUC, I received my B.Eng. (Honors) in Artificial Intelligence from Xi'an Jiaotong University, where I worked with Prof. Nanning Zheng.

My current research interests lie at the intersection of multimodal large language models and graph foundation models. I am especially interested in building methods for multimodal reasoning, structured learning, recommendation systems, and agentic search. I also care about making these systems more effective, robust, and interpretable in real-world settings.
